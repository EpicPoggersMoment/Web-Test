About Pet Simulator 2

How to play: 💰 Collect coins to buy eggs! 🥚 Hatch legendary pets like a Dragon or Unicorn! 🗺️ Unlock and explore new areas & worlds like floating islands! 🔄 Collect and trade pets with other players to become the best! ✨ Upgrade pets or fuse them together!
