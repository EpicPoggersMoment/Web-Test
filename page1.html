How to play BedWars: 📡 Join the "BedWars" queue in the Lobby to find a game 🛌 Protect your base. Once it's gone, you can no longer respawn! 💎 Gather resources to purchase items and team upgrades 🏹 Destroy enemy beds and eliminate players to win the game!
